Principles of Karmyog

Timeless insight, lovingly adapted for our modern lives.

Abhyāsa (Practice)

Create living domains—Health, Svādhyāya (Self-Study), Seva—then show up gently, every day.

Karma & Vikarma

Choose actions that nourish (Karma) and release habits that cloud (Vikarma)—with kindness, not judgment.

Yajña (Offering)

Mark tasks complete by symbolically offering them to the inner fire—turning work into worship.

Sātatya (Consistency)

Celebrate streaks for each practice—steady steps become sacred momentum.
